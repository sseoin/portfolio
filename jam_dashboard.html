Jam Dashboard

Simple, at-a-glance visualization of group analytics

ROLE
UX/UI Designer
INSIGHT
Group admins frequently utilized the CSV exported data to retrieve group metrics. The analysis on the metrics was time consuming due to the nature of tabular presentation of large amount of data. Key metrics would often suffice for the analysis.
We identified the most important and actionable metrics to be the adoption and participation. Group admins utilizes these key metrics to assess the ROI, take corrective actions, and invest or de-invest in group content accordingly. We aimed to enable group admins to view the metrics on numerous platforms without having to export data to a CSV document, post-process the file, and manage reporting data.
arrow_back Previous arrow_forward Next
SOLUTION
The dashboard shows the adoption and participation at-a-glance. The collaboration score and the summary tiles provides simple and easily understood visualization of group health and activity.
In the beginning of the project, I established three principles to guide the design: conversational, transparent, and actionable. Visual breakdown of the score has gone through multiple iterations and user testings to ensure transparency. Dashboard educates and equips group admins by displaying the key metrics and the follow-up steps to improve their group.
