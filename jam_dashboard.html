Jam Dashboard

Simple, at-a-glance visualization of group analytics

INSIGHT
Group admins cannot get group metrics easily because of the static CSV export format. Report data is tabular and takes time to analyze. Often group admins just want a simple, at-a-glance visualization.
We identified the need for group admins to monitor the adoption and participation level of their groups, assess ROI, take corrective actions, invest or de-invest in group content accordingly. Viewing data natively without having to export, post-process, and manage reporting data offline on both web and mobile devices.
arrow_back Previous arrow_forward Next
SOLUTION
I designed a dashboard to allow for viewing the adoption and participation at-a-glance. The collaboration score and the summary tiles on top of the dashboard provides a simple visualization of group health and activity.
One of our design rationale for the dashboard was transparency; we wanted to show breakdown of the score to show 'why' so that users can make sense of the score. Tips and suggestions encourages users to proactively determine what actions to take next.
