SAP Jam Mobile

Collaborate with colleagues across your organization at any time, anywhere.

INSIGHT
SAP Jam is a collaboration tool that helps 34 million subscribers connect with employees, partners and customers. Getting access to key information, process, data, and people should be fast, easy, and intuitive, regardless of where and how your employees are working.
We recognize that customers have a variety of devices, and “BYOD” (bring your own device) is still a popular trend in organizations. Our customers have told us that mobile is key to supporting process transformation.

SOLUTION
We launched SAP Jam mobile applications in both iOS and Android platforms. One of the key goals we had was to create an app with features that help mobile workers – sometimes called “road warriors” – to better collaborate with others and help them feel more connected to the rest of the organization.
We created an experience that is familiar to users, leveraging their experience with our web application. As a visual design lead, I was responsible for establishing a visual design language for both platforms.
