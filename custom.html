Customized home pages for modern intranet

ROLE
UX/UI Designer
INSIGHT
Companies spend a lot of resources creating and maintaining their corporate intranet, the single place where employees get the latest company news and events. IT admins manage information across multiple and diverse business units, update specific pages and publish them on schedule to serve hundreds and even thousands of users.
SAP Jam aims to provide an enterprise collaboration solution thatâ€™s rooted in adding business value while removing complexity, by bringing the right content, people and information together. It also helps IT admins leverage the solution as an intranet supplement or replacement.

SOLUTION
As a lead UX designer, I designed end-to-end solution for IT administrators to create customized home pages. Branded communities earn customer trust and ultimately drive better customer interactions.
In the beginning of development cycle, I hosted 5-day design sprint, and customer interview sessions. I quickly learned that overall experience begins to suffer when the customization is offered via programmatic means, such as HTML and CSS. As a result, Jam Page Designer enables customers to apply their corporate branding, share company-wide news and rearrange layouts in a drag-and-drop fashion. This multi-release strategy has given me opportunities to successfully deliver this complex product, which includes branding, customization, delegation, and translations.
