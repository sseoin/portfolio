Custom Homepage

Customized home pages for modern intranet

INSIGHT
Companies wanted a more prominent way of communicating news and information. To support “enterprise wide collaboration” there is a growing interest for customer to control the company home page.
SAP Jam aims to provide an enterprise collaboration solution that’s rooted in adding business value while removing complexity, by bringing the right content, people and information together. It also helps IT leverage the solution as an intranet supplement or replacement.
arrow_back Previous arrow_forward Next
SOLUTION
As a lead UX designer, I designed end-to-end solution for IT administrators to create customized home pages. Branded communities allow for stronger relationships with better communication, which ultimately helps drive better customer interactions.
This solution consists of branding, customizing homepage, translation. We conducted interviews with over 10 customers to gain insights, and took multi-release approach to gather end-user data and feedback. I hosted 5-day design week with a product manager and engineers to answer some of the critical business questions.
